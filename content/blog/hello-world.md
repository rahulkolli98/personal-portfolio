## Why I Started Writing

I've been building software for a while now — full-stack products, AI integrations, mobile apps — and somewhere along the way I realized that the most valuable things I learned never made it into a README.

Most of them disappeared into the cracks: half-remembered debugging notes, late-night voice notes, and "we should write this down" moments that never got written down.

This blog is an attempt to change that.

## What I'll Write About

Honestly, I don't have a rigid content plan yet, and I like it that way.

This space will probably be a mix of:

- Things I break and what they taught me
- Ideas I am testing in public before they are polished
- Small technical wins that saved me hours
- Notes from building in the middle of real life, not in a perfect lab setup

## What This Is Not

This isn't a tutorial factory. I'm not going to write "10 things you didn't know about React hooks" posts.

I'm more interested in the *why* behind decisions than the *how* — because the how is usually in the docs, and the why is usually nowhere.
